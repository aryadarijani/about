I am actively looking for exceptional Ph.D. candidates interested in the field of Machine Learning (ML). We're seeking passionate individuals with a strong technical foundation, backed by solid math and coding skills, who are interested in leading cutting-edge projects in ML. If you're enthusiastic, forward-thinking, and eager to pioneer innovations in the field, you're the perfect candidate we want to hear from.

Application: The applications are centrally managed by the department (ECE and/or CS) at UW Madison. We invite you to follow the standard procedure outlined by these departments. However, you are welcome to send me an email with your CV, transcript as well as a brief description of your research interests. Unfortunately, given the high volume of applications we receive, it might not be feasible to respond to every email pertaining to PhD applications.

Aligned with UW Madison's priorities, we are committed to promoting inclusion and diversity. We encourage applications from underrepresented groups.
